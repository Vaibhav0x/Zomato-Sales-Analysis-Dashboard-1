Zomato Sales Analysis Report
Zomato Sales-Order Analysis Report

Welcome to the Zomato Sales-Order Analysis Dashboard. This interactive report provides a comprehensive view of sales performance, order trends, and customer behavior within the Zomato platform. Designed for business analysts, sales managers, and decision-makers, the dashboard delivers valuable insights to drive data-informed strategies.

Key Features:

  1. Sales Performance Overview:
    • A dynamic visualization that tracks total sales revenue across different time periods. This includes yearly, helping you identify high-performing periods and spot seasonal trends.
  2. Order Trends Analysis:
    • Detailed charts showing the number of total sales amount, quantity delivered, total ratings from customer and total ordered.
  3. Customer Demographics:
    • Insights into customer demographics, location-based analysis. This section helps in identifying the key customer segments and tailoring marketing strategies accordingly.
  4. Categorization According to Customer Orders:
    • A list of the veg, non-veg and others food category how much food category gains the sales.

How to Use the Dashboard:

  • Interactive Filters: Utilize the interactive filters on the right-hand side to customize the view according to your specific needs. You can filter by date range, location, or specific product categories.
  • Hover for Details: Hover over any data point to get detailed insights and exact figures, helping you make informed decisions.
  • Export Options: Download the data or specific visualizations in various formats (CSV, PDF, etc.) for further analysis or presentations.
